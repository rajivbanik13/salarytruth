## Why SalaryTruth

Most salary calculators give approximate numbers. SalaryTruth uses:

- **EPF Act rules** — PF capped at ₹1,800/month (12% × ₹15,000 wage ceiling)
- **Income Tax Act slabs** — FY2024-25 new and old regime exact calculations
- **Section 10(13A)** — HRA exemption formula with metro/non-metro distinction
- **Standard deduction** — ₹75,000 under new regime (FY2024-25)
- **Professional tax** — state-specific deduction

No estimates. No rounding. No surprises.
